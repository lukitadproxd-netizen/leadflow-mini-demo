# Delivery package

## What the client receives

- A working static contact or booking-request page.
- Mobile-friendly HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files.
- Service/package section with prices or "from" pricing.
- Inquiry form fields agreed in chat.
- Ready-to-send reply draft for each inquiry.
- Local lead history in the browser.
- CSV export for leads.
- Short handoff notes for editing text and deploying.

## What the client must send

- Business name.
- Current website or social profile, if any.
- Main services and prices or price ranges.
- Preferred contact method: WhatsApp, email, form destination, or booking link.
- City/timezone.
- Brand colors or 1-2 reference links, optional.
- Any required wording, disclaimers, or business rules.

## What is not included

- Calls or meetings.
- Payment processing setup.
- Customer accounts or authentication.
- Backend database or CRM integration.
- SMS, email automation, or inbox access.
- Ad campaigns, SEO campaign, or analytics setup.
- Full redesign or custom brand identity.
- Ongoing maintenance after delivery.

## Revision and guarantee

- One small revision round is included within 3 days of delivery.
- Revision covers text, colors, fields, package labels, and minor layout changes.
- If the delivered page does not match the agreed scope, fix it before final handoff.
- New features outside the agreed scope are quoted separately.

## Timeline

- USD 60 contact tune-up: 24 hours.
- USD 75 contact page: 24 hours.
- USD 90 booking request widget: 24-48 hours.

## Suggested collection

- USD 60 job: full payment upfront is acceptable.
- USD 75-90 jobs: 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.
- Payment rail is confirmed manually before starting: PayPal, Wise, AirTM, MercadoPago, bank transfer, or another approved option.
