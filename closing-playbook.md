# LeadFlow closing playbook

Use only after a prospect replies with interest. Do not send payment details or negotiate terms without owner approval.

## Fast qualification reply

Hi {{name}}, thanks for replying.

I can build this as a simple {{niche}} lead flow:

- one mobile-friendly page/widget
- fields for {{fields}}
- ready-to-copy reply text
- CSV export of submitted leads
- one small revision after delivery

Price: USD {{price}}
Delivery: {{eta}} after I receive your content and the upfront payment.
No call needed. We can do everything here by chat.

To start, I would need:

1. Business name and logo, if you want it included
2. Website or social link
3. Services/packages to show
4. Best contact method for new leads
5. Preferred payment method for the 50% deposit

## Scope confirmation

This includes a static lead page/widget and handoff files.

It does not include ads, SEO campaign, backend database, SMS, CRM setup, payment processing, calendar sync, or ongoing support.

## Delivery message

Hi {{name}}, the first version is ready here:

{{demo_or_preview_link}}

Included:

- lead form
- package/service options
- reply draft
- CSV lead export
- handoff notes

Please send any small revision notes in one message. After final payment, I will send the final files and setup notes.

## Follow-up after no reply

Hi {{name}}, quick follow-up on the small {{niche}} lead flow idea I sent.

If useful, I can keep it very small: one quote/request page, delivered in 24h, no calls, USD {{price}}.

If not relevant right now, no worries and I will not follow up again.
