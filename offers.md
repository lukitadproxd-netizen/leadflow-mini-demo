# LeadFlow Mini offers

These offers are designed to be sold and delivered fully async by chat. No calls, no meetings, no payment processing integration, and no invented client data.

## 1. Fast Contact Page

**Price:** USD 75.

**Delivery:** 24 hours after receiving the required client inputs.

**Includes:** one-page mobile-ready contact page, service/package section, inquiry form, ready-to-copy reply draft, basic styling, CSV lead export, and handoff notes.

**Does not include:** backend database, custom brand identity, ads, SEO campaign, payment processing, copywriting longer than one page, analytics setup, or ongoing support.

**Best for:** local service businesses with no website, weak CTA, or a social profile that needs a simple lead page.

**Demo:** `index.html`.

## 2. Booking Request Widget

**Price:** USD 90.

**Delivery:** 24-48 hours after receiving the current site URL and desired fields.

**Includes:** package/service selector, request form, preferred date field, owner reply draft, local lead history, CSV export, and embed-ready HTML/CSS/JS.

**Does not include:** calendar sync, authentication, CRM setup, database hosting, SMS, inbox access, custom API integrations, or payment collection.

**Best for:** businesses that already have a page but lose leads because visitors cannot request availability quickly.

**Demo:** package selector + inquiry form in `index.html`.

## 3. Contact Flow Tune-Up

**Price:** USD 60.

**Delivery:** 24 hours after receiving the website or social link, business type, and preferred contact method.

**Includes:** quick contact-flow audit, sharper CTA copy, improved inquiry prompts, reply templates, simple lead tracker, CSV export, and handoff notes.

**Does not include:** redesign, backend work, AI chatbot hosting, CRM migration, ad management, paid tools, or inbox management.

**Best for:** businesses with an existing site/form that feels unclear, slow, or hard to act on.

**Demo:** reply draft + lead tracker in `index.html`.

## Default terms

- Communication: chat only.
- Delivery: 24-48 hours.
- Payment: 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For USD 60 jobs, full payment upfront is also reasonable.
- Revision: one small revision round within 3 days of delivery.
- Client owns final files after final payment.
- Payment rails to confirm manually: PayPal, Wise, AirTM, MercadoPago, bank transfer, or another method the seller approves.
